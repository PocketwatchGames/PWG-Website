Our Games

Tooth and Tail

Tooth and Tail is an Arcade-Style Real-Time Strategy game set in a world of animal revolution. Wage war with customizable factions, featuring units like mustard-gas skunks and flamethrowing boars.

From the creators of the IGF-winning game Monaco, Tooth and Tail distills the Real Time Strategy genre down to a few buttons and eight-minute matches.

Monaco

Monaco: What's Yours Is Mine is the award-winning single player or co-op heist game. Assemble a crack team of thieves, case the joint, and pull off the perfect heist.

Play with up to four people online or on the same screen. Compete with others via daily leaderboards on Steam. Find out why it won the 2010 IGF and has been described by PCGamer as "one of the best co-op games of all time."

Venture Africa

Explore the Serengeti in Wildlife Tycoon: Venture Africa as Dr. William Willoughby, the poetic ecologist. Build and balance ecosystems of predators and prey by planting vegetation big and small, which feed animals as small as the tiny spring hare and as large as the African elephant! Make sure you don't add too many lions and crocodiles, or they may over-hunt the herds of zebras and wildebeests, or add flocks of vultures and packs of hyenas to fill in the gaps of your ecosystem.

There are no concession stands, janitors, ticket booths, or walls to constrain your animals in Venture Africa. Live free in the wild, and build epic ecosystems to rival the plains of Africa!

Venture Arctic

Venture Arctic reinvents the sim genre and challenges the player to act as Mother Nature, with both terrible and beautiful powers. Use the powers of the sun and fertility to grow food for herds of caribou and pods of whales, and then use the force of the wind and cold to collect the spirits of the deceased so that your polar bears and wolverines can live to see another Summer.

With an art style and musical score inspired by traditional Inuit art forms, Venture Arctic is a game like no other. Feel the rhythms of the far north, and learn to harness the powers of nature as the sun sinks low and the cold takes it's grip.
